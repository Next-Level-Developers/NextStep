# NextStep тАФ Career Universe Database
> **Version:** 1.0 | **Phase:** Brainstorming / Ideation  
> **Target Users:** School (Grade 8тАУ12) & College Students (Year 1тАУ2) in India  
> **Total Careers Listed:** 300+ across 25 domains  
> **Filtering Principle:** Careers are retained if they are (a) discoverable by a student through education/interest, (b) viable in India or entering the Indian market, and (c) explainable to a 16-year-old in one sentence.

---

## Filtering Rules Applied

| Rule | Description |
|---|---|
| тЬЕ Kept | Careers accessible via Indian education pathways (degree, diploma, certification, self-taught) |
| тЬЕ Kept | Careers with a name a student can Google and find Indian job listings or courses for |
| тЬЕ Kept | Future careers (2030тАУ2035) that are emerging in India тАФ flagged with ЁЯФо |
| тЭМ Removed | Highly niche operational roles (e.g. "Septic System Installation Technician", "Overhead Crane Operator") тАФ not career-discovery relevant for students |
| тЭМ Removed | Titles that are sub-roles of a broader career already listed (e.g. "Derrickhand", "Roughneck" under Petroleum Engineer) |
| тЭМ Removed | Luxury/niche industry roles with no India market relevance at student level (e.g. "Superyacht Interior Designer", "Fine Art Courier") |
| тЭМ Removed | Duplicate entries across sections (e.g. VFX Artist, Technical Writer appeared in multiple domains) |
